Insurances & Saving Plans

Our clinic accepts almost every dental insurance and dental saving plan. Indiana insurance companies don't offer stand alone plans for dental insurance. There are some insurance plans available but they need a certain section of care. Also, we accept secondary dental insurance plans to help you get the best coverage for dental treatment. We accept the following dental insurances:

  • Dentemax
  • Envolve
  • Guardian
  • HIP/HIP PLUS
  • Metlife
  • Principal
  • United Health Care
  • United Concordia
  • Aetna
  • Ameritas
  • Anthem
  • BCBS
  • Cigna
  • Careington
  • Dentegra
  • Dentaquest
  • Delta dental
