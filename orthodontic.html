Orthodontics Treatment

The word “orthodontics” arises from the Greek word orthos which means proper or straight, and odons means teeth. This dental specialty is mostly about analyzing and treating tooth misalignment and irregularity within the jaw area. 

Having a bad bite can turn into a major concern if left untreated. It can make it harder for you to chew some foods and can cause muscle tension and pain. Some of the benefits that you get with earlier orthodontics treatment are:

  • Reduces risk of tooth decay
  • Guides adult teeth
  • Assists with speaking
  • Improves digestion
  • Reduces teeth grinding
  • Corrects unhealthy oral practices

    Procedure Of Orthodontic Treatment

    First, the orthodontist will insert a tool to keep your mouth and tongue dry. Then he or she will dry your teeth and spread an etchant to create the tooth surface ideal for bonding. After that, they will place the braces using a special adhesive to hold them in situ throughout your treatment.

    Earlier, orthodontic treatment was taken into account in teens and pre-teens. At present, around 30 percent of them are suffering from orthodontics. 

    Although the treatment works at any age, the American Dental Association believes people should take an orthodontic assessment at the age of seven.

    Make sure to bring your kids for the orthodontic assessment. In case they are diagnosed with that, we can start the treatment soon.

    Treatment Period

    Some treatments may take a couple of months, while others may take several years. It completely depends on the complexity of your orthodontic needs. However, the treatment takes between 12 and 36 months.
