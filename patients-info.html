INDUSTRY

Welcome To The Best Place For Your Dental Concern

Let Us Help You With Your Oral Health Issue

We can be the best choice to look out for dental services of exceptional value. We offer blankets and pillows, digital X-rays, and iTero digital impressions, and for a few procedures, we use the Epic Biolase Laser. Our focus is to make you feel comfortable during your appointments. You can opt for evening and weekend appointments according to your convenience.

When Come Up To Us

  • According to the American Dental Association, everyone should get their dental assessment done every 6 months to get away with any major dental concerns. 

What To Do On Your First Visit

  • Arrive 15 minutes earlier to complete the document work.
  • Make sure to provide your accurate personal, dental insurance, and medical/dental health history; so we can suggest the appropriate information.
  • We need to see your documents from the previous dental clinic; so we can provide you with the best treatment.

  • Approval from a legal guardian/parent is required for a patient under 18 years of age to proceed with any dental treatment.

  • Feel free to reach out to us if you have any doubts. We will be glad to guide you in making the right decisions.

We Greatly Appreciate You For Being Supportive

We want to ensure our patients receive excellent dental care.

  • Try to arrive at our clinic 15 minutes before, so you have the time to make any changes to the documents.
  • Do not hesitate to ask any questions regarding dental health and our services.

We are thankful that you chose us to serve your dental needs, and we will keep working towards providing you with an amazing experience. In case you have any queries, get in touch with our doctor and staff. Our Warmest Greetings,Smiles By Gentle Dental, Griffith, IN.
