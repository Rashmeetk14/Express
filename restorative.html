Restorative Dental Services

Having a missing tooth can take a toll on your confidence. When you have ways to get away with it, why wait? With our one-of-a-kind restorative services, we can help you get your confidence as well as smile back.

Our dental specialists have helped a number of patients to get away with their missing teeth. You can also take advantage of our restorative services.

We have various options to restore natural teeth; some of them are

  • Aesthetic nonmetal crowns
  • Bridges
  • Silver restorations
  • Tooth-colored resin composite restorations.
  • Veneers
  • Flipper or acrylic temporary partial.

Fillings

Dental fillings are one of the most sought-after options when it comes to restorative dentistry. This is used to fill the decayed portions of your teeth that are caused by cavities. We use the proper filling material for your teeth that will provide the looks and function of an intact tooth.

With the expertise of Dr. Brar, you can get a dental filling that will look exactly like your real teeth.

The dental fillings are available in different materials; you can discuss them with the dentist and move on with the one that suits the best for your tooth.

Get your tooth restoration done by the expert and smile with more confidence.

Crowns

We also offer crowns- they are a tooth-shaped cap that is applied on a damaged, decayed, or cracked tooth. Crowns give a proper size, aesthetic, shape, and strength to the damaged tooth.
